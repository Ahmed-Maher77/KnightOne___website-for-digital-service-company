Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, development process, and support options. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A basic website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while complex applications may take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation and maintain regular progress updates throughout development.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. Our support includes regular maintenance, security updates, performance monitoring, and technical assistance. Enterprise clients receive 24/7 priority support with dedicated account managers.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We specialize in modern web technologies including React, Angular, and Vue.js for frontend development; Node.js, Python, and Java for backend services; and cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. Our team stays current with emerging technologies to provide cutting-edge solutions.
